Publications

Conference papers
UAV-BASED CLOSE MONITORING OF THE VEGETATION FOR PRECESION AGRICULTURE PURPOSES:
In this paper, we have developed a cost-effective aerial monitoring system for surveying vegetated areas by modifying regular RGB cameras to obtain NDVI data with higher resolution than available satellite images. The paper has been accepted and is scheduled for presentation at the upcoming Mediterranean and Middle-East Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium 2024 (M2GARSS 2024).

Risk Assessment Model for Algerian Wildfires:
(GitHub)
This paper presents a dataset on wildfires in Algeria and introduces a straightforward neural network model as an example for predicting future occurrences. The paper has also been accepted and is scheduled for presentation at the upcoming Mediterranean and Middle-East Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium 2024 (M2GARSS 2024).
